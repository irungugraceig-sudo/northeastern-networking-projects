üîß Generic Mapping Procedure (GMP)

GMP Stuffing Calculation

GMP enables efficient transport of client signals that don't align perfectly with OTN tributary slot rates. For the 22 Gbps client in 10 OPU3 TSs:

Stuffing Analysis: Available capacity: 23.621 Gbps Client rate: 22.000 Gbps Excess capacity: 1.621 Gbps Per-frame stuffing: Frame duration: 3.238 Œºs Client bits per frame: 71,236 bits Available TS bits per frame: 76,480 bits Stuff bits per frame: 5,244 bits Stuffing ratio: 5,244 √∑ 76,480 = 6.86% overhead

Why GMP Matters

  • Flexibility: Supports non-standard client rates without dedicated containers
  • Efficiency: Minimizes bandwidth waste through dynamic bit stuffing
  • Standards: Enables transport of emerging high-speed interfaces (25G, 50G, 400G)
  • Cost-effective: Single OTN infrastructure handles multiple client types
